A Hero in Swimming: Michael Phelps

Interest Hero

Michael Phelps

One of the greatest heroes in swimming is Michael Phelps, an American swimmer who is the most decorated Olympian of all time, with 28 Olympic medals (23 gold, 3 silver, 2 bronze).
He dominated the sport from the early 2000s to 2016, setting numerous world records and revolutionizing competitive swimming.
Phelps' most iconic achievement was winning eight gold medals at the 2008 Beijing Olympics, breaking the previous record of seven held by Mark Spitz.
His dedication, mental toughness, and extraordinary physical attributes (such as his long wingspan and efficient stroke technique) made him a legend in the sport.

  • Born

    • June 30, 1985 in Baltimore, Maryland, U.S.

  • Training

    • Phelps has trained under Bob Bowman since he was 11 years old.

  • World records

    • Michael Phelps broke 39 world records during his swimming career, the most by any swimmer in history.
