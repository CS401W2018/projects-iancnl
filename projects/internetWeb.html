ASCII is a way of turning letters, numbers, and symbols into numbers that computers can understand. Unicode is similar to ASCII and it is bigger,expect letter, number, and symbols. It can be emojis as well.

UTF-8 is a way to store text on computers.It is part of Unicode, meaning it can handle all letters, symbols, and emojis.
