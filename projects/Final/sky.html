Skydiving is often associated with excitement and freedom, but it's natural to wonder about its risks.
While it is an extreme sport with potential dangers like parachute malfunctions or hard landings, modern safety measures,
training, and technology have greatly minimized these risks. Like any activity, smart decisions and proper precautions go a long way.
Ultimately, the biggest risk might just be falling in love with the thrill of skydiving!
