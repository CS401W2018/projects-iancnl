Friend Request From
    A Friend:
  • Put yourself out there by joining social activities or groups
  • Take initiative by starting conversations
  • Suggest plans to hang out or do something together
  • Be genuinely interested
  • Be vulnerable when appropriate
  • Share your own experiences but keep it balanced
  • Be approachable
  • Find common ground or mutual hobbies
  • Offer help or support when needed
  • Give it time
How to be friend(from online)
    A Good Friend:
  • is there for you, no matter what
  • doesn't judge you
  • doesn't put you down or deliberately hurt your feelings
  • is kind and respectful to you
  • is someone whose company you enjoy
  • is loyal
  • is trustworthy and willing to tell you the truth, even when it's hard for you to hear
  • laughs with you
  • sticks around when things get tough
  • makes you smile
  • is there to listen
  • comforts you when you cry.
A good friend reference(from online)
    A Boy Friend or Girl Friend:
  • Listen to Your Partner
  • Connect Daily
  • Practice Non-Verbal Communication
  • Understand Attachment Styles
  • Learn Each Others Love Language
  • Respect Their Mental Health
  • Show Empathy
  • Offer Validation
  • Fight Fair
  • Structure Your Conversations
