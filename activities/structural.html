Strutural Tags

Header,Nav, Main, Footer

What goes in the header?

Search bar, Website Title, Logo, Navigation Menu, Call-to-Action Buttons, etc.

What goes in the Nav? Can the Nav go in the header? Does it have to go in the header? Does the Nav contain links to another website?

different tags,link, etc goes in Nav. It can go under the header or above the header. It depend on the web designer want to put Nav in the header or not. But it does put Nav in Header Yes, the Nav contain links to website if we want to.

What goes in Main?

Main is the main content part of you website, everything you want goes in Main like Headings, Primary Content, Images, Videos and table, etc.

What goes in the Footer?

Footer is the bottom part of the website, you can put Logo, Copyright Information, social media links, Contact Information
