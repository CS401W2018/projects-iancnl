Box Model

What is the difference between block tags and inline tags?

Block tags (div, p) take up the full width and start on a new line, while inline tags (span, a) only use as much space as needed and stay in line with other content.

What is the margin, border, and padding?

Margin is the space outside an element, border is the outline around it, and padding is the space between the content and the border.

What are units of measurement you can use in CSS?

CSS supports absolute units (px, cm, in) and relative units (%, em, rem, vw, vh).

When would you use a figure tag?

Use a figure tag to wrap images, diagrams, or other media, often with a figcaption for descriptions.
